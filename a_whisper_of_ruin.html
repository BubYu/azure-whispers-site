A Whisper of Ruin

Genre: Romance / Billionaire Romance

After losing everything in a business scandal orchestrated by her fiancé, an ambitious young architect accepts a position designing an opulent resort for an enigmatic billionaire. What begins as a professional relationship quickly turns into a dangerous dance of power, secrets, and passion. But as she discovers the dark underpinnings of his wealth—and a shocking connection to her past—she must decide if love can survive betrayal.
