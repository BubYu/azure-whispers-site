The Midnight Court

Genre: YA Fantasy Romance

Every decade, a secretive fae court invites one human girl to compete for a favor that could change her life. When a bold seventeen-year-old is chosen, she enters a glittering world of danger and deceit. Forced to navigate deadly trials and rivalries, she forms an unexpected bond with the aloof prince who seems equally trapped by the court’s machinations. But winning the favor means unearthing the court’s darkest secrets—and risking everything for the boy she can’t have.
